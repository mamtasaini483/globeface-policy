Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 11, 2026

Welcome to GlobeFace, operated by Advik Digital Studio. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require Name, Email, or Phone Number for login. We collect only the following:

  • Date of Birth: To ensure age-appropriate content and compliance.
  • Profile Picture: To represent your account on the platform.
  • Unique Chat ID: A system-generated ID that allows users to find and connect with you.
  • User Content: Photos, Videos (Reels), and Stories uploaded by you.

2. How We Store Your Data

  • Server Storage: All content is securely stored on Firebase servers.
  • Stories: Automatically expire after 12 hours as per app functionality.
  • Chats: Messages are stored to enable real-time communication.

3. Permissions

We only request permissions that are necessary for app functionality:

  • Push Notifications: For new messages, likes, and important updates.

Note: We do not access your Camera or Gallery unless you explicitly choose to upload content.

4. AI Content Moderation & Safety

To maintain a safe and respectful community, we use Artificial Intelligence (AI):

  • Automatic Scanning: Titles and Descriptions are analyzed during uploads.
  • Abusive Language Filter: Detects hate speech and prohibited content.
  • Content Restriction: Violating content may be blocked or restricted.

5. Data Processing for Safety

Text data such as Titles and Descriptions is processed in real-time strictly for moderation and security purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Firebase (Google): Hosting, database, and backend services.
  • Service Providers: Analytics and performance monitoring tools.

7. Data Deletion & Retention

  • Post/Reel Deletion: Deleted content is removed from active databases.
  • Automatic Deletion: Stories disappear after 12 hours.
  • Account Deletion: Users can permanently delete their account via app settings.

8. Children's Privacy

GlobeFace is intended for users aged 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
